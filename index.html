Yashaswita Bhoir

Yashaswita Bhoir is a current Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Wayne State University, where she will be advised by Dr. Amiangshu Bosu. Prior to joining WSU, Yashaswita worked in the industry as a Systems Engineer with major American Automotive OEMs on their Automotive Telematics programs for Autonomous Vehicles. She has also worked on introducing Connectivity in retail vehicles via mobile apps and voice assistants like Amazon Alexa and Google Home. Many of Yashaswita's projects have been coached as and further implemented in Agile environments. Yashaswita takes keen interest in issues related to Diversity & Inclusion in Software Engineering as well as Agile Coaching of Software teams. Leave a message for me at yashaswita@wayne.edu!
